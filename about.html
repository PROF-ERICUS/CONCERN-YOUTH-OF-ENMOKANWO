About Us

About Us

Concern Youth of Enmokanwo (CYC) is a youth-driven organization committed to unity, empowerment, and community development. We create opportunities that uplift youth and strengthen our society.

Vision

To build a united community where every youth is empowered to contribute meaningfully to sustainable development.

Motto

"Unity, Service, and Progress."

Core Values

  • Integrity: Honesty, transparency, accountability.
  • Unity: Togetherness drives growth.
  • Service: Humility in serving the community.
  • Leadership: Empowering youth to lead change.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards.

Empowerment

We offer capacity building, skill development, and leadership training enabling youth to take charge of their future.

Mission

To nurture a generation of responsible, skilled, and united youth through education, training, and community service.
