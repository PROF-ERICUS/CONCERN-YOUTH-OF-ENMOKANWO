About Us

Concern Youth of Enmokanwo (CYC) is a community-driven youth organization dedicated to empowering young individuals through leadership, education, and service. Our vision is to unite the youth towards building a better society where opportunities and growth are shared.

Since our establishment, we have engaged in numerous community development projects, outreach programs, and youth empowerment initiatives. We believe that through unity and service, we can bring positive change to our people and beyond.

Join us in our mission to inspire, develop, and transform lives through meaningful programs and activities tailored for the youth.
