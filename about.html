About Us

Concern Youth of Enmokanwo (CYC) is a youth-driven organization committed to unity, empowerment, and community development. We are passionate about creating opportunities that uplift the youth and strengthen our society.

Vision

To build a united community where every youth is empowered to contribute meaningfully to sustainable development.

Motto

"Unity, Service, and Progress."

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions.
  • Unity: We believe in the power of togetherness and collective responsibility for growth.
  • Service: We are dedicated to serving our community with humility and selflessness.
  • Leadership: We inspire and nurture responsible leaders who can drive positive change.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.

Empowerment

We provide platforms for capacity building, skill development, and leadership training to enable youth to take charge of their future and make a positive impact.

Mission

To nurture a generation of responsible, skilled, and united youth through education, training, and community service initiatives.
