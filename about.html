About Us

The Concern Youth of Enmokanwo is a vibrant community association formed to unite and empower the young people of Enmokanwo. Since 2018, we have been committed to fostering growth, promoting education, and building a strong sense of togetherness.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for the youth through education, skills development, cultural preservation, and community-driven initiatives that bring positive change.

Our Vision

To see Enmokanwo become a united and thriving community, where the youth are empowered, engaged, and actively shaping a brighter future for all.
